About Us

Welcome to House of Frenchies, your dedicated source for exceptional French Bulldogs. We are a family-owned business deeply passionate about French Bulldogs and committed to their well-being.

Meet Hernandez Family

In a cozy corner of Edinburg, nestled among blooming gardens and quaint streets, lies a home filled with wagging tails and endless love for French Bulldogs. Meet Daniel, a lifelong enthusiast of these charming companions, whose journey into the world of Frenchies began with a single furry friend named Ginger.

Two years ago, Daniel discovered a deep admiration for French Bulldogs while searching for a loyal companion to join their family. Little did they know, this decision would spark a lifelong passion and commitment to these unique dogs. With Ginger by his side, he embarked on a mission to share the joy and companionship that French Bulldogs bring to countless homes.

Driven by a desire to raise French Bulldogs of exceptional quality and temperament, Daniel and his wife founded House of Frenchies. What started as a humble endeavor in their backyard soon blossomed into a renowned haven for French Bulldog lovers across the nation. With a meticulous approach to breeding and a heartfelt dedication to each pup, House of Frenchies became synonymous with trust and excellence in the Frenchie community.

But House of Frenchies is more than just a breeding business—it's a family affair. A longside Deniel, and his wonderful wife Jacquelin, and there beautiful children they play an integral role in nurturing and caring for every Frenchie that enters their home. Each pup is raised with love and attention, ensuring they grow into happy, healthy dogs ready to bring joy to their new families.

Today, as you step into House of Frenchies, you're greeted not only by the adorable faces of their French Bulldogs but also by the warmth and passion that define their journey. Whether you're seeking a loyal companion, a show-quality Frenchie, or simply a place to share your love for these wonderful dogs, House of Frenchies invites you to become a part of their story.

As they continue to write new chapters filled with love, dedication, and a deep-rooted passion for French Bulldogs, Deniel and his family remain committed to upholding the legacy of House of Frenchies—one wagging tail at a time.
